# Privacy Policy — Sudz

**Last updated:** March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **Sudz** (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account & profile

- **Name** — to identify you and personalize the app.
- **Email** — for account creation, login, and communication.
- **Phone number** — for verification, login, and service coordination.
- **Profile image** (optional) — if you choose to upload one.
- **Locale / language** — to show the app in your preferred language.

### 1.2 Service & booking

- **Addresses** — delivery or service addresses you save.
- **Booking details** — service type, date, time, and any special requests.
- **Payment-related data** — processed according to our payment providers’ policies (we do not store full card numbers).

### 1.3 Technician use (if you use the app as a technician)

- **Job assignments** — jobs assigned to you and their status.
- **Arrival, start, and completion** — timestamps and status updates.
- **Photos / evidence** — pre-work, post-work, or rejection evidence you upload (subject to limits set in the app).
- **Offline actions** — actions performed while offline, stored locally until synced with our servers.

### 1.4 Device & app usage

- **Device information** — such as device type and OS version, to support and improve the app.
- **Local storage** — preferences, cached profile data, and pending sync data stored on your device.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account and profile.
- Process and manage service bookings, orders, and packages.
- Coordinate and track technician jobs (arrival, start, complete) and sync offline actions.
- Verify your identity (e.g. phone verification).
- Communicate with you about your account, bookings, and support.
- Improve our services, fix issues, and ensure security.
- Comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms.

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored on our servers and, where relevant, temporarily on your device (e.g. for offline use).
- We use industry-standard measures to protect your data (encryption, access controls, secure APIs).
- Evidence photos and sensitive data are transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS).
- Passwords and authentication tokens are handled in a secure manner.

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information only:

- **With service providers** that help us run the app (e.g. hosting, analytics if we use them), under strict confidentiality.
- **When required by law** — to comply with legal process or authorities.
- **With your consent** — for purposes we clearly explain when we ask.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their marketing.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction** — ask us to correct inaccurate data (you can also update your profile in the app).
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational requirements.
- **Object or restrict** — object to or request restriction of certain processing.
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a portable format where applicable.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in the “Contact” section below.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Sudz is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of important changes in the app or by email where appropriate. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 8. Contact

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy or your data:

- Use the **Contact** or **About** section in the Sudz app (if available).
- Use the contact information published on our website or app store listing.
- Reach out to our support or data protection contact as provided by the app operator.

---
